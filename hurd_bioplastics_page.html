Hemp Hurd for Bioplastics

Bio-Based Inputs for the Circular Economy

Our micronized hemp hurd is refined to create bio-fillers and bio-resins that reduce reliance on petroleum-based plastics. This material enhances strength, flexibility, and biodegradability across multiple polymer platforms.

It is ideal for packaging, automotive components, consumer goods, and compostable materials.
