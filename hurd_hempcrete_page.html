Hemp Hurd for Hempcrete

Low-Carbon Construction with Natural Materials

Our hemp hurd is specially processed to provide excellent binding, breathability, and insulation when combined with lime-based binders. It is used for casting structural walls, preformed blocks, and prefab panels.

Hempcrete improves thermal performance, resists mold and pests, and contributes to long-term carbon storage in the built environment.
